Yongfeng Zhong

I am currently working at Aibee Inc. as a tech lead and responsible for building the pipeline for large-scale computer vision applications for smart shopping malls, airports, etc. Before that, I was a senior research engineer at IDL Baidu Research and worked on fine-grained image recognition. My research outputs have been applied to several influential online apps, such as Nuomi App, covered by VentureBeat.

I studied in the Department of Computer Science, University of Bonn, and obtained a master's degree in computer science, advised by Prof. Dr. Sven Behnke. Before coming to Germany, I obtained my master's degree in information science at The Institute of Science and Technical of China (ISTIC). I received my Bachelor's degree in Information Management and Information System at Wuhan University.
